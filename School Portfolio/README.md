DIAMONDO - Car Dealership, Rental, and Service App
Overview
DIAMONDO is a mobile-first car dealership, rental, and service booking platform designed to solve the common problems people face in the traditional car industry—such as time-consuming car searches, non-transparent pricing, and inefficient booking systems.

It unifies all car-related needs in a single, user-friendly interface and uses a pink-themed design to reflect the company’s values of love, care, and emotional engagement.

Key Features
Smart Car Listings – Browse cars by type, price, and location.

Real-Time Rental Booking – Find available rentals and book instantly.

Service Scheduler – Book diagnostics or maintenance directly from your phone.

AI Chatbot Support – Get immediate assistance for common queries.

User Profile Management – View bookings, transaction history, and preferences.
