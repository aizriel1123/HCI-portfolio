Tap "Buy" to access categorized listings (e.g., sedan, SUV, used).

Car Rentals
Tap "Rent" to view available vehicles near your location.

Filter by duration, type (e.g., economy, premium), and distance.

Confirm rental by selecting date and time; receive in-app confirmation.

Service Booking
Tap "Service" to book car services (e.g., diagnostics, oil change).

Choose your preferred date and service center.

A confirmation message and booking status are shown on the dashboard.
